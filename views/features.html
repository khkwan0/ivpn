VPN is an acronym for ‘Virtual Private Network’, historically used by private organizations to link geographically diverse locations seamlessly over the internet. Ignite VPN "tunnels" traffic between two devices by transmitting it over the public internet. Internet Security

Ignite VPN acts as a bodyguard for all the data you send over the internet. Once connect, your device is assigned a new IP address—owned by us, not your Internet Service Provider. All of your traffic is encrypted and tunneled to our server. Once there, it is decrypted and allowed to travel to its intended

destination. Anyone snooping will only see a single encrypted data stream between you and our server—nothing else.

If you use your laptop or mobile device at WiFi hotspots, hotels, or other public networks your connection is potentially under risk by identity thieves and other hackers. Ignite VPN encrypts all traffic between your device and our servers thereby giving you a very strong level of protection.
