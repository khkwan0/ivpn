Frequently Asked Questions

What is VPN?

VPN is an acronym for ‘Virtual Private Network’, historically used by private organizations to link geographically diverse locations seamlessly over the internet. Ignite VPN "tunnels" traffic between two devices by transmitting it over the public internet. Ignite VPN takes this one step further by giving you the option to encrypt all the data you send and receive, and additionally allows you to appear to be located many miles away or in a different country from where you really are.

What can I do with a VPN?

A VPN is a multi-purpose digital survival tool. You can use your VPN to: Hide your real IP address and surf the web with absolute privacy Protect your confidential information (e.g. e-banking credentials, social media passwords) through VPN encryption Unblock and access your favorite websites and social media platforms Stream and download anything securely, anonymously, and with no limits Get discounts when you shop online

Why do I need a VPN?

You need Ignite VPN if you don't want websites, your internet provider, or hackers having access to your personal information. Ignite VPN will encrypt your data while it's in transit, making it unreadable for third parties. IgniteVPN will also hide your IP address and location. Ignite VPN can also be needed if you need to access blocked or censored content. You will have access to 190 different locations which will give you the freedom you desire.
