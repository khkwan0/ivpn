Manual Setup

Set up your VPN on a Mac (OS X)

Open Settings

Open your computer’s System Preferences then open Network. Click on the “+” sign under the menu on the left.

For the Interface, choose VPN. For VPN Type, select L2TP over IPSec. For Service Name, type in the name you’d like to use. Click Create.

  • Server Address: Type the desired IgniteVPN server address. (Or use 23.253.99.109)
  • Account Name: Type your username you registered with.
Click on Authentication Settings.

  • Password: Type your password you registered with.
  • Shared Secret: Type engagebdr
Click on Advanced.

Under Options, check the box for Send all traffic over VPN connection. Click OK.

Check the box for Show VPN status in menu bar. The VPN icon will appear in your menu bar in the upper-right corner of your screen. Click Apply to set the VPN configuration.
Connect to the VPN server location

You can connect to IgniteVPN either by clicking Connect in the Network settings window, or by clicking on the VPN status icon in the menu bar and selecting Connect [your network name].


Congratulations! You can now begin surfing with freedom and security! Open your web browser, and all traffic will automatically be protected by IgniteVPN.
